Scrap Skorpion
---
I came across an image of this scrap metal recreation of the Skorpion SMG on Pinterest and instantly knew I wanted to recreate it in 3D. I made a few design changes to make it a functional weapon and fit into a post-apocalyptic setting, including internals.
---
Blender, ZBrush, Substance Painter, Photoshop, Marmoset
